# Product

## Register

product

## Users

System admin (primary) manages the platform: creates users, assigns roles and permissions. Secondary users access the app with role-scoped capabilities. Both are internal operators, not end-customers. Context: desktop, work hours, task-focused.

## Product Purpose

Zafeer is an internal Arabic business management system for Gulf/KSA companies. It centralizes employee management (residency documents, payroll, deductions, transfers), company and project tracking, alerts, and reporting. Success = admin completes daily HR operations without friction; nothing lost, nothing missed.

## Brand Personality

Reliable, precise, professional. The tool should feel like a trusted colleague: it doesn't surprise you, doesn't waste your time, and gets things right. Arabic-first, RTL-native.

## Anti-references

- Generic American SaaS look (HubSpot, Intercom): gradient heroes, identical card grids, hero-metric templates.
- Heavy ERP density (SAP, Oracle): information overload, no visual hierarchy.
- Consumer-mobile aesthetic: oversized touch targets, playful color, tablet-first padding on desktop.
- Data-dashboard maximalism: charts everywhere, neon accent colors, BI-tool vibes.

## Design Principles

1. **Correctness over decoration**: every visual element earns its place by communicating something, not decorating it.
2. **Arabic-native**: RTL layout is the default, not an afterthought. Arabic type hierarchy comes first.
3. **Operational clarity**: the current task, its status, and the next action are always obvious without hunting.
4. **Trusted density**: information-dense without feeling crowded. Professional tools can hold more than consumer apps.
5. **Predictability**: consistent patterns, no surprises. Admins return daily; they need muscle memory, not rediscovery.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Arabic screen reader support. Sufficient contrast for dense data tables. No reliance on color alone to convey status (use icons + labels alongside color).
